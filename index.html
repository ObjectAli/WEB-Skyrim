ABOUT DRAGONBIRD
Many people have heard the term "Dragonborn" - of course, because we are ruled by "Dragonborn Emperors" - but few people fully understand its meaning. Most scholars agree that the term was first used in connection with the Akatosh's Covenant. "Akatosh looked at the calamities of people and expelled the priceless blood from his own heart, and blessed Sacred Alessia with this blood of the Dragons, and made the Covenant that as long as the descendants of Alessia are faithful to the dragon blood, Akatosh will keep the seal on the Oblivion Gates and will remove the Daedra and Undead armies from the enemies of their Ayleids, who are honoring the Daedra. " Those whom Akatosh blessed with "the blood of dragons" were called Dragonborn.
